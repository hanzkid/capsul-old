SHIPPING & PAYMENT


Once your order has been placed you will recieve an email verifying your payment and another email regarding delivery and tracking number. Orders will be shipped within 3 to 5 business days.

Due to co-vid 19 delays may occur depending on your area / Country and delivery service. Please contact your delivery service with tracking numbers for more detailed information. Local orders will be made by JNE and international orders by DHL or FED EX tracking details will also be updated once item/s have been shipped. Once you've recieved your shipping details, please feel free to contact them for further information regarding your shipment.
